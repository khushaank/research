Data & Privacy Policy

Effective Date: September 18, 2025

Data We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us when you create an account. This includes your email address, full name, designation, and any profile photo you choose to upload. We do not collect any personal information beyond what is necessary for account functionality.

How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely for the purpose of personalizing your experience on our website (e.g., greeting you by name) and managing your account. Your profile information, including your avatar and name, is publicly visible to other users. Your email address is kept private.

Data Storage and Security

All user data is securely stored and managed by our backend provider, Supabase. We rely on their industry-standard security measures to protect your information.

Deleting Your Account

You have the right to request the deletion of your account and all associated data at any time. To do so, please follow the instructions on your account settings page to send a deletion request to our support email.
