Frequently Asked Questions

How can I cite a research paper from this site?

We recommend using the APA citation format. You can find all necessary details, including author, title, and publication date, at the top of each paper. For example: Gupta, K. (2025). Predictive Modeling of Crop Yields... Khushaank Gupta Researches.

Is the data used in your studies available for download?

Yes, we are committed to open science. Each paper includes a "Links & Sources" section at the bottom where you can find links to the datasets used, typically hosted on open data portals or our public GitHub repository.

Can I collaborate on a research project?

We are always open to collaboration with academic institutions, fellow researchers, and industry partners. Please reach out to us via the contact email above with a brief proposal of your ideas.
