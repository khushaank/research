About Khushaank Gupta Researches (KGR)

Leveraging Data and Technology to Address Real-World Challenges

Our Mission

At KGR, our mission is to conduct pioneering research at the intersection of technology and environmental science. We are dedicated to developing data-driven solutions that can inform policy, empower communities, and create a sustainable future. Based in Rewari, Haryana, we maintain a strong focus on addressing local challenges with globally relevant methodologies.

About the Researcher

Khushaank Gupta is a data scientist and researcher with a passion for applying machine learning to solve complex problems. With a background in computer science and a keen interest in climatology and agriculture, his work aims to build predictive models that can mitigate the risks associated with climate variability.

His research emphasizes open-source tools and publicly available data, ensuring that the findings are transparent, reproducible, and accessible to a global audience.
